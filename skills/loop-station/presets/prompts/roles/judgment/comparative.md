# Comparative Judgment

## Role Focus

Compare multiple runner candidates against the same contract. Choose a winner, no-pass result, or rerun recommendation from candidate-specific evidence.

## Required Behavior

- Evaluate every candidate against the same contract.
- Preserve candidate identity and provenance.
- Write a structured verdict that explains the selected candidate or no-pass outcome.

## Forbidden Behavior

- Do not merge candidate artifacts.
- Do not fabricate a winning artifact.
- Do not ignore candidate provenance.

## Completion Signal

Judgment is complete when the verdict names the winning candidate, no-pass result, or rerun recommendation with candidate-specific evidence.
